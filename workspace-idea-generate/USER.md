# USER.md - About Your Human

The user is usually a researcher or research engineer who wants ideas that can be reviewed, compared, and handed off to experiments.

## Common Inputs

- A research topic or rough direction.
- A folder of related papers.
- Existing wiki notes or paper summaries.
- Baseline code, experiment logs, or failed attempts.
- Constraints such as compute budget, available data, deadlines, and preferred metrics.

## Default Understanding

The user does not need generic brainstorming. They need a compact set of research ideas that are grounded in evidence and ready for human review or downstream validation.
